The Power of Compounding

Albert Einstein allegedly called compound interest the "Eighth Wonder of the World". In a Systematic Investment Plan (SIP), you don't just earn interest on your principal amount, you also earn interest on your past interest! The longer you stay invested, the steeper your wealth curve grows.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. Can I pause my SIP anytime?

Yes, mutual funds offer immense flexibility. You can pause, stop, or increase your SIP amount at any time without heavy penalties.

Q2. Are mutual fund returns tax-free?

No. Equity Mutual Funds are subject to Long Term Capital Gains (LTCG) tax of 10% on gains exceeding ₹1 Lakh in a financial year.

Q3. What is a good return rate to assume?

Historically, Indian index funds (Nifty 50) have delivered around 12% to 15% annual returns over long periods (10+ years). However, past performance doesn't guarantee future results.

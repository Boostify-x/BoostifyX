Guide

What is a QR Code?

A Quick Response (QR) code is a two-dimensional barcode that stores digital information. It can be easily read and decoded by smartphones, tablets, and dedicated QR scanners like Google Lens.

Why does a QR code need a white background?

Scanners rely on high contrast to detect the small square modules inside a QR code. Our tool automatically adds a "Quiet Zone" (a solid white border margin) around the code. This ensures that no matter what app you use to scan it, it works 100% of the time.

FAQs

Do these QR Codes expire?

No, static QR codes generated on our platform are permanent and do not have an expiration date. They will work as long as the content (like your website link) remains active.

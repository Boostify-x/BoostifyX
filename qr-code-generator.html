Guide

What is a QR Code?

A Quick Response (QR) code is a two-dimensional barcode that stores digital information. It can be easily read and decoded by smartphones, tablets, and dedicated QR scanners. Originally invented in Japan for the automotive industry, QR codes are now used globally to connect the physical world with digital spaces.

Benefits of Using a QR Code Generator

Generating a custom QR code offers multiple benefits for businesses and personal use:

  • Marketing & Menus: Print QR codes on business cards, flyers, or restaurant tables so customers can visit your website or digital menu instantly without typing a URL.
  • Wi-Fi Sharing: Convert your complex Wi-Fi password into a QR code. Guests can simply scan it to connect to your network.
  • Contactless Payments: Commonly used in digital wallets like Google Pay, Paytm, and Apple Pay to initiate quick and secure transactions.

Are QR Codes Permanent?

Yes. The QR codes generated by the BoostifyX tool are Static QR Codes. This means the data is permanently encoded into the image itself. It will never expire and will continue to work forever, completely free of charge.

FAQs

Do these QR Codes expire?

No, static QR codes generated on our platform are permanent and do not have an expiration date. They will work as long as the content (like your website link) remains active.

Is it safe to scan QR codes?

QR codes themselves are just text. However, always ensure you trust the source where the QR code is printed, as scanning malicious codes could direct you to phishing websites.

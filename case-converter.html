Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is Title Case?

In Title Case, the first letter of every single word is capitalized. It is mostly used for Book Titles, Headlines, or Names (e.g., "The Quick Brown Fox").

Q2. What is Sentence case?

In Sentence case, only the very first letter of the sentence is capitalized, and the rest of the text is converted to lowercase (just like normal human writing).

Q3. What is Alternating Case?

Alternating case randomly or sequentially changes letters to upper and lower case. It is popularly used on the internet to indicate sarcasm or memes (e.g., "hElLo wOrLd").

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2026

At Boostifyx.in, accessible from https://boostifyx.in, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Boostifyx.in and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We respect your privacy. Boostifyx.in does not store or save any of the personal data, files, texts, or images you upload. We do not require you to create an account or provide an email address to use our free tools.

2. Local Processing (Client-Side)

Almost all of our tools (such as Image Compressors, Calculators, and Formatters) operate entirely within your browser using JavaScript. This means your data is processed locally on your device and is never uploaded to our servers.

3. Local Storage & Cookies

We use your browser's Local Storage strictly to track anonymous tool usage (to display the "Trending Tools" feature on our homepage). This data is completely anonymous and stays on your device. We may also use third-party cookies for advertising purposes.

4. Third-Party Ads

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use aggregated information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, please contact us.

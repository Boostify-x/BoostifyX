Guide

How does a Timezone Converter work?

Because the Earth is a sphere, the sun rises and sets at different times in different parts of the world. To standardize this, the world is divided into 24 main timezones. A timezone converter calculates the exact mathematical difference between your location and a target location.

Popular Timezone Differences:

  • IST to EST: India is exactly 9 hours and 30 minutes ahead of Eastern Standard Time (New York).
  • IST to GMT: India is 5 hours and 30 minutes ahead of Greenwich Mean Time (London).

This tool is essential for scheduling international Zoom meetings, tracking Forex and Stock Market openings, or watching global eSports tournaments without messing up the time.

FAQs

Does it handle Daylight Saving Time (DST)?

Yes! Our converter uses modern JavaScript localization APIs which automatically adjust for Daylight Saving Time changes happening in the US, UK, and Australia.

What does "+1 Day" mean?

Because of massive timezone gaps, a meeting scheduled at night in America might actually fall on the "Next Day" (Morning) in India. Our tool explicitly tells you if the day changes.

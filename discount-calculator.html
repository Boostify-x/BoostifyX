Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. How is the discount calculated?

We simply calculate the percentage of the original price. E.g., a 20% discount on ₹100 means you save ₹20, and the final price becomes ₹80.

Q2. What does "50% + 50% Off" mean in shops?

It does NOT mean the item is free (100% off). It means they first give a 50% discount on the MRP, and then give another 50% discount on the new reduced price. The actual total discount is 75%.

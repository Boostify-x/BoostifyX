Security Guide

Why You Need a Strong Password

In today's digital world, hackers use automated software to "brute-force" or guess passwords. If your password is a dictionary word (like "monkey123") or your name, it can be cracked in less than a second.

A truly strong password generated by the BoostifyX Password Maker combines uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols to create a mathematical wall that would take computers millions of years to crack.

What makes a good password?

  • Length: It should be at least 12 to 16 characters long. Length is more important than complexity.
  • Unpredictability: It should not contain birthdays, pet names, or consecutive numbers like "1234".
  • Uniqueness: Never reuse the same password across multiple websites. If one website gets hacked, all your accounts are at risk.

FAQs

Are my generated passwords saved anywhere?

No! This tool runs entirely on your device's browser using local JavaScript (Client-Side). Your passwords are never sent to our servers.

How do I remember these random passwords?

We highly recommend using a reputable Password Manager (like Bitwarden, 1Password, or Google Password Manager). You only need to remember one "Master Password", and the app will remember all these complex passwords for you.

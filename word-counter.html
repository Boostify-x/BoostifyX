Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. Are spaces counted as characters?

Yes, standard character counts include spaces, punctuation marks, and symbols. If you only want to count letters, many specific tools exist, but standard practice includes spaces.

Q2. How is reading time calculated?

The average adult reads at a speed of about 200 to 250 words per minute. This tool estimates reading time by dividing your total word count by 200.

Q3. Is there a character limit?

No! Unlike Twitter (280 limit) or SMS (160 limit), our tool has no limits. You can paste thousands of words (even a whole book chapter) to count it instantly.

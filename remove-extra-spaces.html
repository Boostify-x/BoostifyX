Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. Why does copied text have so many spaces?

When you copy text from PDF files, Word documents, or poorly coded websites, hidden formatting characters (like tabs and line breaks) get copied too. Our tool strips them away safely.

Q2. Will it delete my regular single spaces?

No. It only targets "double" or "multiple" consecutive spaces and turns them into a single correct space between words.

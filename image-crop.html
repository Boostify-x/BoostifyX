Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is an Aspect Ratio?

Aspect ratio is the proportional relationship between a picture's width and height. For example, 1:1 is a perfect square (ideal for Instagram profile pics), while 16:9 is a wide rectangle (ideal for YouTube thumbnails).

Q2. Is there any loss of quality when cropping?

No, the selected area remains in its original high resolution. We crop exactly what you select without compressing the quality.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is CGST and SGST?

When you sell a product within your own state, the GST is divided into two equal parts: Central GST (CGST) goes to the central government, and State GST (SGST) goes to the state government.

Q2. What are the common GST slabs in India?

The standard GST tax slabs in India are 5%, 12%, 18%, and 28%. Different products fall under different categories.
